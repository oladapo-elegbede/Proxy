# PROXY
### Institutional Navigation for Neurodivergent Learners

**Live Demo:** https://proxy-kappa-pearl-66.vercel.app

---

## The Problem

The systems built to support neurodivergent learners require exactly the executive-function skills those learners struggle with most.

To receive accommodations for executive function impairment, a student must:
- Know which office to contact
- Know what documentation to gather
- Know how to describe their needs in institutional language
- Navigate a multi-step bureaucratic process
- Advocate for themselves under stigma and anxiety

These are precisely the capabilities most impaired in the students who need the system most.

**Fewer than 40% of neurodivergent students successfully access accommodations. The barrier is not awareness — it is the advocacy burden.**

PROXY removes that burden entirely.

---

## What PROXY Does

PROXY is an AI-powered institutional navigation system.

A student describes what is hard — in their own words, no forms, no clinical language required. PROXY identifies their functional barriers, maps them to the support they are legally entitled to, and generates a complete, step-by-step pathway through their institution's accommodation process.

At every step, PROXY tells the student exactly what to do and exactly what to say.

---

## How It Works

**1. Describe what is hard**
The student writes freely. No dropdowns. No checkboxes. No diagnosis required.

**2. PROXY understands**
AI identifies functional barriers and maps them to appropriate academic adjustments using a validated Knowledge Graph of 15 barrier types and 16 accommodation categories.

**3. Follow your pathway**
A personalised, institution-specific step-by-step pathway appears. One action at a time. PROXY holds the map.

**4. Know what you are entitled to**
Every accommodation the student is legally entitled to is listed in plain language — what it is, and what actually changes in practice.

**5. Know what to say**
For every conversation step — disability services, professors, advisors — PROXY generates a personalised preparation script: how to open, what to say, what to do if it gets hard.

## The Core Insight

> The system built to help neurodivergent learners was designed around the assumption that the person who needs accommodation can advocate for themselves. That assumption is wrong.

PROXY does not teach students how to navigate the system. It navigates the system for them.

## Technical Architecture

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 App Router
- **AI:** Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) for barrier identification, pathway generation, and conversation preparation
- **Knowledge Graph:** Structured JSON database of 15 functional barriers, 16 accommodation types, and 3 institution profiles — validated against real institutional policies
- **State:** Zustand with localStorage persistence
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with custom design tokens
- **Deployment:** Vercel

### AI Pipeline

Student description (natural language) ↓ Intake Pipeline (Groq) → Identifies functional barriers → Maps to Knowledge Graph → Returns structured barrier data ↓ Pathway Pipeline (Groq) → Queries institution process from Knowledge Graph → Generates personalised step sequence → Returns complete pathway object ↓ Conversation Architect (Groq) → Generates personalised preparation scripts → Calibrated to student's specific situation


### Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph is the core innovation. It is not generated by AI — it is a curated, validated database that the AI reasons over.

Every accommodation recommendation is traceable to:
- A specific functional barrier description
- An institution's actual process documentation
- A legal policy basis (ADA Title II, Section 504)

This prevents hallucination and makes every recommendation auditable.

---

## Neurodivergent Design Principles

Every interaction was designed against these principles:

1. **Zero prerequisite knowledge** — the student never needs to understand the institution
2. **Zero forms before trust** — no intake form before the system listens
3. **One next step at a time** — never overwhelming, never ambiguous
4. **Shame has no home here** — no clinical language, no judgment, no "why didn't you register earlier"
5. **Student owns all data** — nothing sent anywhere without explicit approval
